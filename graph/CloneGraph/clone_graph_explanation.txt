133. Clone Graph 

Companies: Google , Amazon , Microsoft

Marked: Medium 
seems like: Easy

site: LeetCode 
branch: Top Interview Questions
category: Graph 


** intuition:
 We need to Traverse The inputted Graph To get all nodes and their neighbors.
 and need a map to trac a node is previously cloned or not??

** Traversal Technique 
Here, we can go with DFS or BFS.
i choose DFS.

** Complexity:
Time: 
 O(V+E)
where, V=Number of Nodes.
       E=Number of Edges.

why not O(V*E)???

explanation: For Each node weed need to Traverse neighbors seems like nested loop . but we need to traverse neighbors just at once in total.

Space:
 O(V) because of Using Extra space Unordered_map.
 where,
 V=number of node .
 in this case V has range [0,100].
 so we can write in the case of most worst case
 O(101).
 
 tips: you can also optimize it using hashMap. try it.
